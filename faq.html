Frequently Asked Questions

Where will the 2024 ALAA Conference be held?

The 2024 Australasian Legal Academics Association conference will be held at Flinders University's new city campus. Opened only in February 2024 the new campus is located at the heart of Adelaide's CBD and is adjacent to the Adelaide Railway startTransition, the Festival Centre and Parliament House.

When will the 2024 ALAA Conference be held?

The 2024 Australasian Legal Academics Association conference will be held from July 3 - 5 2024.

When do registrations open?

Registrations open soon!

Register your interest and we will contact you as soon as registrations open.

What accommodation options are recommended?

There are many accommodation options to choose from all within a very short distance from Flinders' new city campus. Here are some of our suggestions:

  • Oval Hotel Adelaide

I need some information about the Gala Dinner

The Gala Dinner will be held on the evening of 4 July.

Gala Dinner will be a formal sit-down dinner. Presentations for the ALEA awards will be made during the dinner.

Pricing and location will be announced soon.
